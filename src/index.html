About Ibbadulrrahman E-Academy

Ibbadurrahman E-Academy is an Islamic educational program that aims to bring Islamic knowledge close to interested individuals. This is a major step towards helping Muslims across the globe fulfill the prophet's command to seek knowledge of the religion and understand its text.

Why you should enroll

100% online

All lectures are held virtually during weekends i.e. Saturdays and Sundays

Flexible classes to suit your schedule

Scheduled time 12 months – 3 Semesters
Maximum allowable time 30 months – 7 Semesters

All lectures are recorded

All lectures are recorded and thereby accessible after each lecture
